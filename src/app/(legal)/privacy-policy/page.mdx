# Privacy Policy for Love MBTI

**Effective Date**: January 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to **Love MBTI** (恋愛MBTI) — a personality assessment platform that helps you discover your love style through the 16 personality types framework. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your personal data when using our Love MBTI service.

## Information We Collect and How We Use It

### 1. **Quiz Responses and Personality Assessment Data**

   - **What We Collect**: Your responses to personality assessment questions, calculated personality type results (e.g., ENFP, INTJ), and timestamps of when assessments were taken.
   - **How We Use It**: To generate your personality type, provide personalized love style analysis, compatibility reports, and relationship insights. Your responses help us understand patterns and improve our assessment accuracy.
   - **Storage**: Quiz results are stored securely in our database. You can request deletion of your results at any time through your account settings or by contacting us.

### 2. **Account Information (Optional)**

   - **What We Collect**: If you choose to create an account: email address, username, password (encrypted), and profile information you provide.
   - **Purpose**: To save your assessment history, provide personalized recommendations, enable premium features, and send you updates about your results (with your consent).
   - **Note**: You can take the basic assessment without creating an account. Anonymous results are stored temporarily and can be accessed via a unique link.

### 3. **Usage and Analytics Data**

   - **What We Collect**: Pages visited, assessment completion rates, features used, time spent on site, referring websites, and general geographic location (country/region level).
   - **Purpose**: To understand how users interact with our service, improve user experience, optimize our personality assessment questions, and measure service performance.
   - **Tools**: We use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that may collect data through cookies and similar technologies.

### 4. **Device and Technical Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Device type, operating system, browser type and version, IP address, screen resolution, and language preferences.
   - **Purpose**: To ensure compatibility across devices, troubleshoot technical issues, prevent fraud and abuse, and deliver localized content.

### 5. **Cookies and Local Storage**

   - **What We Use**: Cookies and browser local storage to remember your preferences (theme, language), maintain login sessions, and track anonymous assessment progress.
   - **Purpose**: To provide a seamless experience, remember your settings between visits, and understand usage patterns.
   - **Your Control**: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit certain features.

### 6. **Payment Information (For Premium Features)**

   - **What We Collect**: If you purchase premium features (detailed reports, couple compatibility analysis): billing name, email, payment method details, transaction ID, and purchase history.
   - **Purpose**: To process payments, provide premium content, manage subscriptions, prevent fraud, and comply with financial regulations.
   - **Security**: Payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers (Stripe). We do not store complete credit card numbers on our servers.

### 7. **Communications**

   - **What We Collect**: If you contact us: your name, email address, and the content of your messages.
   - **Purpose**: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and address your concerns.

## How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With vetted third-party vendors who help us operate our service (hosting providers, analytics services, payment processors, email services). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

- **Aggregated and Anonymized Data**: We may share statistical insights (e.g., "60% of ENFP users prefer..." ) that do not identify individual users for research, marketing, or partnership purposes.

- **Legal Requirements**: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests; to enforce our Terms of Service; to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property; or to protect users and the public from harm or illegal activities.

- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

- **With Your Consent**: We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## Data Retention

- **Anonymous Assessments**: Results from assessments taken without an account are retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted.
- **Account Data**: If you create an account, your data is retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request account deletion at any time.
- **Legal Obligations**: We may retain certain data longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., dispute resolution, fraud prevention).

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Employee training on data protection

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to improve our protective measures.

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information (right to be forgotten).
- **Portability**: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Objection**: Object to processing of your personal information for certain purposes.
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw previously given consent for data processing.
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the link in our emails.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
